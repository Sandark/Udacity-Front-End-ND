Cats are cute!

It's widely known that cats are so adorable when they are young. They are so pleasant with their big heads and large eyes. And adult cats also realize that kittens are harmless and deserve to be cherished.

Here I've collected several reasons why cats are so cute:

1. Cats are all over

Everyone knows that it's hard not to meet cats in the Internet. By statistics pet owners prefer cats over dogs and other pets.

2. Communication with people

Usually cats don't mew at other cats. This cute way of communication is reserved only for humans. This is sweet because cats know more than 100 vocal sounds while dogs can make only around 10.

I always talk to my cat and it helps me deal with everyday stress.

3. Cat's "fingerprint"

Cats also have a "fingerprint". Cat's nose is rigged with with unique pattern. If you have multiple cats, try to compare them!

You can find more information about how your cat is unique in my another article.

5. Cats have awesome nightvision

Cats can't see fine detail or rich color, but have a superior ability to see in the dark because of the high number of rods in their retina that are sensitive to dim light. As a result, cats can see using roughly one-sixth the amount light that people need.

It's awesome how great cats are. But don't forget that the most important is to show your pet love and care!
